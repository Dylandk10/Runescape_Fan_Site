Welcome to DK OSRS

Welcome

This website was made by Dylan Kelly for the massive multiplayer online game known as Old School Runescape. Runescape was release January 4th, 2001. However Old School Runescape (OSRS) was released February 22nd, 2013. The purpose was to bring back the old feel of the game before the massive updates like no PVP (player vs player) in the wilderness and before the combat update. The game has over 70,000 players active at a time. There is tons to do from skilling, player vs enviorment, player vs monster and player vs player. There are over 100 quest and a stable economy. This website was made to help players learn more about OSRS and to discover items, monster and players in the game.

Check it Out!

This websites offers great features for current, old and new OSRS players. By clicking the button SEARCH OSRS you can search for in-game items, monsters and players. If you search for a player you can compare two players at a time to see who has better stats, monster kills, mini-game points, quest points and player vs player kills. This is all taken from the offical Runescape website. I DO NOT OWN ANY OF THIS INFORMATION AND IT IS ALL COPYRIGHT BY JAGEX! So get out there and discover the game we love!
